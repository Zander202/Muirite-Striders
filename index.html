THE CLUB

About Us Muirite Striders Running Club is a dynamic running community in Uitenhage where passion for running and community come together. We welcome runners and walkers of all ages and fitness levels — from beginners to experienced athletes. Our focus is to motivate one another, promote healthy living, and achieve goals together in a friendly and supportive environment. At Muirite Striders, it’s not just about running — it’s about teamwork, discipline, and community spirit. One Stride. One Team. One Community.
